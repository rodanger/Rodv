Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about our web development and IT services. Find out how we can help your business grow.

1. What is included in the Basic package?

The Basic package includes website development, logo design, and deployment. It's perfect for startups or small businesses looking to establish an online presence.

2. Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, our Standard, Advanced, and Premium packages include ongoing IT support. We offer 24/7 assistance for technical issues and website maintenance.

3. Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time to a higher package to include additional features such as Meta Ads setup, SEO optimization, or full customization.

4. Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, all our packages include design services, but Advanced and Premium packages allow for full custom solutions tailored to your brand and business needs.

5. How long does it take to launch a website?

Timeline depends on the package and complexity. Basic websites usually take 1–2 weeks, while Advanced and Premium websites with custom features can take 3–6 weeks.
